[Academics] Vision-Servo Underwater Gripping Robot

YIBO ZHOU published on 12/30/2022 Fri
1 Duration 06/00/2021 - 12/00/2022 2 Overview The Underwater Grabbing Robot is designed for 2021 National Innovation Training Project for University. I was responsible for the circuit system design, control algorithm design, and visual servoing algorithm design of the robot. Unlike traditional frame-based underwater grabbing robots, this robot features a four-axis olive-shaped structure that effectively reduces fluid resistance, giving it high maneuverability. The bottom gripper is capable of visual servoing to grasp target objects.

[Academics] Vision-Servo Agricultural Quadcopter

YIBO ZHOU published on 11/20/2021 Sat
1 Duration 07/00/2021 - 11/00/2022 2 Overview The Vision Servo Agricultural Quadcopter is the project I designed for National Undergraduate Electronics Design Contest. It ultilizes TM4C123G as the main controller. By using inertial sensors, laser ranging sensors, and optical flow sensors for perception, the UAV enables closed-loop control. Additionally, I employed a camera for visual servo. The control algorithm is based on a dual-loop PID controller, allowing for stable flight and good maneuverability.
