2 Overview

During the summer vacation of Sophomore Year, I got the opportunity to join the Machine Intelligence Laboratory (MiLAB) in Westlake University as a visiting student for 8 month (from July 2022 to March 2023). MiLAB is founded Dr. Donglin Wang. They conduct the pioneering research of Robot Learning, with the mission to endow real robot with high flexibility, fast adaptation and autonomous learning by making use of machine learning algorithms. The specific topics they focus on are: 1) Deep Reinforcement Learning Theory; 2) Deep Meta-Learning Theory; 3) Robot Behavior Intelligence.

Upon being assigned to the Quadruped Blind Guiding Robot project by Dr. Wang, I conducted the systematic review on the related works. After I restored the algorithm codes from previous works, I found the issues of the existed blind guiding systems. To solve the problem, we proposed a data-driven guiding system to cope with the effect brighten by individual differences among users. We compared the performance of our models with previous works and achieve significant performance improvements. Our guiding system was deployed in both simulation and real quadruped robots. Finally, I composed our works to a research paper, and submitted it to IEEE Conference.
